Privacy & Terms

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Data Collection

We do not require user registration or accounts to use this website. We collect personal data in only one way:

  • Contact Form: When you message us, we collect your name and email address. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is never shared with third parties or used for marketing without your explicit consent.

2. Media & Photography

Yardley Muslim Centre is a vibrant community hub. We occasionally take photographs or videos during public events, prayers, and lectures to share updates on our "News" and "Events" sections.

  • We aim to respect the privacy of all attendees. If you see an image of yourself or a family member that you would like removed, please contact us via the website form, and we will take it down promptly.

3. Donations & Financials

This website displays the Mosque's official bank details for direct Sadaqah and Lillah transfers. Please note:

  • We do not process payments directly on this server.
  • Security: Always verify bank details through official Mosque channels if you are unsure. We are not responsible for transfers made to incorrect accounts or third-party fraudulent sites.

4. Usage Terms

By using this site, you agree to the following:

  • Prayer Times: While we strive for 100% accuracy in our timetables, prayer times are provided as a guide. Please allow for slight variations.
  • Content: All content, including Quranic quotes and event descriptions, is for informational and spiritual purposes.
  • Prohibited Use: Users must not attempt to bypass the admin security or use the contact form for spam or malicious intent.

5. Cookies

This website is designed to be privacy-first. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics to monitor your personal browsing habits.
