The Heart of the Community

Alhamdu Lillah

A Milestone for Yardley

After years of prayers and hard work, Allah (SWT) has blessed the Birmingham Muslim Foundation (BMF) to establish the first Masjid & Madrasa on Stoney Lane. Occupying the complex at 47-51 Stoney Lane, the Yardley Muslim Centre (YMC) is being transformed into a multi-functional hub to serve the spiritual and educational needs of our community.

Renovation in Progress

Work is currently on-going to repurpose this former commercial site into a dignified place of worship. We are currently hosting Jumu'ah and Taraweeh prayers as we build towards full completion.

The Complex

A £1.2 Million investment to secure a permanent spiritual home for Muslims in South Yardley.

We are looking for 1,200 brothers and sisters to contribute £1,000 each to become founding members of the community.
